Research Mission

My research focuses on developing novel techniques for dynamic, non-blocking load balancing; designing scalable work-stealing algorithms for GPUs and distributed clusters; and optimizing memory access patterns to maximize system throughput.

Beyond these core areas, I maintain a broad interest in High-Performance Computing, parallel architectures, hardware architecture, operating systems, and computer networks. Essentially, any domain that centers on large-scale system design and performance optimization.
